Coronado Bridge,
San Diego

Mmm. San Diego. Drink it in. And this is the Coronado Bridge. Don't act like you're not impressed. It's 2.12 miles of pure concrete, with a vertical clearance of 200m, so that even the tallest of ships can pass beneath it. And by the beard of Zeus, crossing it by car is a delight.
