Lotusland,
Santa Barbara

This 37-acre Santa Barbara garden treasure is my pride and joy. As a girl I was an opera star, known for my voice which according to my critics sounded like "five million pigs" - which I'm sure they meant as a unique compliment. But horticulture was my real vocation, and I remained head gardener here at the most famous exotic garden in the US until I passed on to the great greenhouse in the sky aged 97.

Here in Santa Barbara you can also join the urban wine trail and gallivant from watering hole to watering hole sampling some of the finest Californian wines.
