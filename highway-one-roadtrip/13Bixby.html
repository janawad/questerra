Bixby Bridge

Or at least it used to be…This bridge - the highest single span bridge in the world - is named for Bixby Creek which is named for yours truly, Charles Henry Bixby, of Livingston County, New York. Pleased to meet you too. The Bixby Bridge is the gateway to the legendary Big Sur. It's sometimes known as "Rainbow Bridge" for the supporting arch of the bridge, first crafted from 300,000 board feet of Douglas fir, which locals thought looked like a rainbow, and for the multi-coloured carpet of wild flowers that surround the bridge.
