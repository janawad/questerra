Venice Beach

One of the world's most famous workout spots and the stomping ground of
bodybuilding champions. No surprise that I've made a few
appearances here, then.

On the famous boardwalk you'll encounter snake charmers, buskers,
masseuses, hippies for whom it still is and will always be 1972.
The equally ubiquitous roller-bladers, skaters and tribal drummers vie for
dominance of the beach front. You can't miss the skate park, with its huge
graffiti wall, where tagging is always permitted to prevent vandalism
elsewhere, and the volleyball courts, which have starred in many
a cheesy eighties pop video.
