Monterey

The Pebble Beach links is every keen golfer's dream course. The dramatic coast line and forest make even the shoddiest swing look epic. Clint Eastwood, Teddy Roosevelt, Sir Winston Churchill, Jay Leno, Bing Crosby and Bill Murray have all played here.

But Monterey isn't just about golf. The region's also famous for its wildlife and its picture-perfect natural scenery. On the famous waterfront street, Cannery Row, you'll find the Monterey Bay Aquarium where you can see sea otters, penguins and have the chance to feed sharks. Monterey's rocky coast is purpose-built for the sea otters who cavort amongst the surf and feed on the kelp forests. You can pay them a visit via kayak.
