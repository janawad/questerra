Hollywood Sign

You know what? I almost did. I bought the land surrounding the "H" in the Hollywood sign. Up there I'd intended to build my very own love nest for my beloved, Ginger Rogers, and myself. That fell through when she left me, but still… I'm known for being what you might call a flagrant spendthrift, and alongside aviation, Hollywood was my favourite playground.

The Hollywood sign officially became a cultural landmark in 1973. It's now perhaps the most iconic piece of signage in the world, but back in the seventies it fell into disrepair and celebrities like Alice Cooper and Hugh Hefner clubbed together to fund its upkeep.
