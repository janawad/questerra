Fort Bragg

C'mon, don't just sit out here in the brine with your perm frizzing to oblivion. Fort Bragg is a fine spot for fishing, whale watching, kayaking, twitching and cold water diving, if that's your thing. There's also a fine-looking working harbour - the one where I suffered my little mishap - plus the Skunk Train, an historic logging line and the glass beach, composed of squillions of sea-polished glass pebbles.

That's a cheap souvenir, if you ask me.
