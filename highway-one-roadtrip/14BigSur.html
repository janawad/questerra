Big Sur

So, Big Sur's this, like, totally far out vibe. I mean, it's not even, like, a place. It's an experience really. A state of mind. Once upon a time, cats like Jack Kerouac, Henry Miller and the Beat generation came here to freak out and found their muse. But even now you'll find kindred spirits here. There're no traffic lights, banks or strip malls here. It's totally mellow.
