Eureka

'Eureka' means 'I have found it!' and although the Gold Rush miners that christened our fine city have long since skedaddled and there's no more gold left in them there hills, you can bet your sweet life this historic locale is still a bonanza. Keep your best one peeled for original 19th century homes in the Old Town Historic District and I hear there's much lushery up at Carson Mansion, if you catch my drift.
