Kanchipuram

1) Detailed architecture: Definitely a kingsly town that has over 108 intricate temples favouring Lord Vishnu.

2) The town is world renowned for its silk craftsmanship.

3) The town is a living example of master architecture dating back to the dynasties of the Pallavas and the Vijayanagar empire ( approx 275 AD)

4) Was the Capital of the Pallava Dynasty

5) Chinese philosopher & Traveler Xuan Zhang bought in relevant Buddhist influence here too.
