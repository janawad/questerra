Madurai

1) Madurai has been a major settlement for two millennia and is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world.

2) With 15000 visitors a day and 60 million in annual revenues pay your respects to the one of the more frequented temples in India: Meenakshi temple, concentric Quadrangular outook, 4 entrances facing 4 directions and many more interesting facts to it.

3) The temple enclosure also boasts of 1000 pillar corridor.

4) Industrial and educational hub in south tamil nadu.
