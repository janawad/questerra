Tiruchirapalli

1) Also known as Trichy, it houses the notable Rock Fort, Ranganatha Swamy temple and Jambukeswarar temple.

2) The world’s oldest surviving dam built in the Chola era is present here.

Fun Fact: Trichy exported large quantities of “Cheroot” a brand well known by the 19th century british cigar smokers.
