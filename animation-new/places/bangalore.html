Vellore

1) Welcome to Vellore, which carries the history of the famed Indian Goddess of wealth, whose throne will enchant you with its sparkling gold architecture and exuberance.

2) The temple is ornate with up to 10 layers of gold foil, the intricate design detail of gold is hand crafted and inspired by the Vedas.

3) Before you get to the magnificence of the deity, you have to maneuver your path around a star shaped maze.
