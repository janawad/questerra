Thanjavur

1) Way before the advent of cement and other binders Thanjavur boasts buildings without any kind of binders and is laid just by having granite blocks atop each other. The temple also has over 100 secret tunnels of passages in its block for security maneuvers.

2) The main temple also is astonishingly over 1000 years of age and is a “hollow” temple which rests upon its head an 80 Tonne carved stone structure!

3) Named “Rice bowl” of tamil nadu, because of agricultural activities in the region of river Cauvery.

4) Gold Rush!! Boasts a world famous style of painting that has originated from Thanjavur which is using gold in its painting called Tanjore Painting.

5) Spatika lingam is one the 12 rarest of rare gem stone lingas of Lord Shiva (most powerful Indian deity)
