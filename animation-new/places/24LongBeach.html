Queen Mary Hotel,
Long Beach

Long Beach is one of the largest ports in the world, and the point of entry for most goods imported into the west side of the United States. Here you'll find my vessel, RMS Queen Mary.

She's an old girl; a legendary liner which after an illustrious career as a high society cruise ship was retired in 1967. These days she's a popular tourist attraction and also a favourite stop off for those seeking the weird and the wonderful. Recently featured on Most Haunted and Scariest Places on Earth, and voted one of the top 10 most haunted places in the USA, the ship is said to be home to at least 600 ghosts
(including me).
