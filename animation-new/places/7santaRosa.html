Santa Rosa

Snoopy and I called Santa Rosa home for over 30 years. Now you can visit the Charles M. Schulz Museum, which holds the largest collection of Peanuts comic strips in the world, skate in my rink, Snoopy's Home Ice and make a pilgrimage to Snoopy's famous red kennel.

It's a kick-back kinda place, and if you fancy a tipple, you're in luck too. This friendly and unassuming town is the gateway to the Napa Valley, which those in the know reckon is one of the top wine growing regions in the world. Some say the vineyards and mountains are best viewed from above, in the basket of a hot air balloon.
